Our Story

Isihlabani Trading and Projects has been registered in 2017 as a formal business that will deliver various services to the local municipality and further enter the private sector as well as the government sector. This was based on the decision by the management to try entering the mainstream of the economy. It identified a gap in the market to supply its services that were much needed by the local community and local municipality. The management felt that they could start a business that will deliver these services at a reasonable cost to their customers and be able to create employment for himself and later on people in the community. It is envisaged that this business will grow from small operation to the business that will employ many people and grow on yearly basis. The business will thrive in the fact that it will create a rapport with it's clients who often refer business to them. Currently the business is looking for avenues to grow it's revenues clientele. The management are hands people and we are reasonable extensive experience in business management. We are exceptional good at our work and extensive clients satisfied with our work.

QUALITY POLICY STATEMENT

Our mission is to provide quality workmanship and efficient service delivery while imparting skills that enables sustainable livelihoods for the communities around us while enhancing stakeholder confidence and benefits and also promoting company image and brand.

VISION

Our Vision is to become the preferred supplier of quality and best service in the construction industry and to our customers at all times.

VALUES

We envision being one of the most influential entity in the industry ;with integrity as a foundation of all our undertakings, both in the private and public sector .To embark on projects that will lead to skills -impartation to women ,the youth and the disadvantaged ; to promote job creation and impact lives as one of its core priorities ; while being one of the most sought after entity in the construction and management of civil projects due to our excellence.

MISSION

To be the first choice service provider in the Interior design work. We aim at creating a well established institution that not only sustains relevance and excellent service in the Republic of South Africa but also resonates with the African continent as a whole.
