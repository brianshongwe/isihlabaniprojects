Services

Civil Construction

We specialized branch of civil engineering that focuses on the design, building, and maintenance of the physical and natural infrastructure essential for society, including projects like roads, bridges, dams, airports, water systems, and buildings.

Electrical Installation and Maintenance

We specialize in design, setup, and ongoing care of electrical systems, covering everything from wiring and outlets to lighting and equipment to ensure safety, reliability, and efficiency.

Building Construction

We offer a wide array of services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. These include new home builds, major and minor renovations, extensions, and general maintenance

Sewer Maintenance

Specializes in drain cleaning, High-pressure jetting, CCTV camera drain inspections, and septic tank services, sewer repairs, and general plumbing clears blocked sewer drains and gullies using machines and rods, and offers sewer repairs, general plumbing, drain cleaning, and leak detection.

Construction of Manholes and Kerbings

We specialize in building underground access points (manholes) for drainage and utility systems and installing kerbs (kerbings) along roads, sidewalks, and other paved areas. This includes excavation, installation of precast concrete or cast-in-place structures, pipework, backfilling, and surface finishing, ensuring proper alignment, stability, and safety.

Paving

We specialized in installation design maintenance and repair of paved surfaces like driveways, patios, walkways, and roads using materials such as concrete, asphalt, and bricks, for residential, industrial and commercials industries.
